Instruções

A - Adicionar cubos (clique do mouse) 
S - Selecionar cubo (clique do mouse)
X - Remover cubo selecionado
R - Modo de rotação (clique do mouse)
T - Modo de translação (clique do mouse)

Instructions

A - Add cubes (right mouseclick)
S - Select cube (right mouseclick)
X - Remove selected cube
R - Rotation mode (right mouseclick)
T - Translation mode (right mouseclick)
